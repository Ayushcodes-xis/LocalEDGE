Bistro 74: From Empty Tables to Fully Booked Weekends

                     
            
              

The Challenge

              

                Located in the highly competitive Hauz Khas area, Bistro 74 was struggling to attract weekend footfall despite exceptional food and premium interiors. Their digital presence was virtually non-existent, forcing them to rely entirely on unpredictable walk-ins.               

            
            
              

Our Approach

              

                We deployed a high-performance website with a frictionless native reservation system. Simultaneously, we completely overhauled their Google Business Profile for local SEO dominance and launched highly targeted Meta ad campaigns focusing strictly on the South Delhi radius.               

            
            
              

The Result

              

                Within four months, waitlists became the standard for Friday and Saturday nights. Search visibility skyrocketed, making them the #1 organic result for "fine dining Hauz Khas".

              #1               Organic Google Ranking
